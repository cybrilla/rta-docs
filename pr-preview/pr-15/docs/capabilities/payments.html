Payment modes and their settlement TATs​

Payment ModesDay of Debit to Investor's AccountDay of Unit allotmentRemarks
Netbanking/UPI (Basket orders)TT+1
Netbanking/UPI (single order payments)TT/T+1- If order placed before cut-off time, then T day NAV, else T+1 day NAV.
- Dependent on AMC support of banks.
Please refer to Same-day settlement supported banks per AMC.
UPI AutopayTT+1
eNACH MandateTT+1

Note:

  • Cut-off for liquid funds is 1:15PM and for non-liquid funds is 2:45PM.
  • NAV applicability is subject to realisation of funds in AMC collection account.
  • NAV is only applicable on a market working day.
