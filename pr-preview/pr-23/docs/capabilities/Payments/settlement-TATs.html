Payments Settlement TATs

Payment modes and their settlement TATs​

Payment ModesDay of Debit to Investor's AccountDay of Unit Allotment
Netbanking/UPI (Basket Orders)TT+1
Netbanking/UPI (Single Order Payments)TT/T+1**
UPI AutopayTT+1
eNACH MandateTT+1

Note:

  • ** For single-order payments, the same day settlement is subject to support of the payment methods and banks by the given AMC as detailed in the below table.

Note on NAV applicability:

  • For single-order payments, the day of unit allotment depends on the time of order submission. If the order is placed before the fund’s cut-off time, units are allotted at the T-day NAV; otherwise, allotment occurs on T+1.
  • The fund cut-off time for liquid funds is 1:15 PM, and for non-liquid funds is 2:45 PM.
  • NAV applicability is subject to realization of funds in the respective AMC’s collection account.
  • For basket orders, the day of unit allotment is always T+1.
  • Unit allocation is limited to market working days only.
